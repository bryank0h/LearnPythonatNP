Speed Training Mode

Welcome to Speed Training Mode. This is the place where you test the skills that you have learned, and polish them against time! Challenging against time may sound tough, but this is indeed the best method to activate your minds and work on problems like never before. Do these questions like your life depended on it, and your programming skills will eventually elevate to greater heights!

Easy Mode        : 10 randomized MCQ questions, 30 seconds per question.
Standard Mode : 10 randomized Fill-in-the-blank questions, 1 minute and 30 seconds per question, only for those who wants a real challenge!

Easy Mode

Instructions:

  1. There are 10 MCQ questions.
  2. You have 30 seconds to answer each question.
  3. Select an answer before clicking on "Submit".
  4. Points are awarded, and the faster you answer, the more points you earn.
  5. You can always stop the training and return to the selection page.
  6. If you get the maximum points (3000 points), a certificate will be shown on screen for you to take a screenshot!
  7. Do your best!

Standard Mode

Instructions:

  1. There are 10 Fill-in-the-blank questions.

  5. Points are awarded, and the faster you answer, the more points you earn.
  6. You can always stop the training and return to the selection page.
  7. If you get the maximum points (25000 points), a certificate will be shown on screen for you to take a screenshot!
