Speed Training Mode

Welcome to Speed Training Mode. This is the place where you test the skills that you have learned, and polish them against time! Challenging against time may sound tough, but this is indeed the best method to activate your minds and work on problems like never before. Do these questions like your life depended on it, and your programming skills will eventually elevate to greater heights!

Easy Mode         : 10 randomized MCQ questions, 30 seconds per question.
Standard Mode : 10 randomized Fill-in-the-Blank questions, 1 minute and 30 seconds per question, only for those who wants a real challenge!

Choose a mode to view the respective leaderboard. The leaderboard is ranked according to the number of points earned, followed by the timing.
